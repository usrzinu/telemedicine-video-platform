Tips for a Great Consultation

Make the most out of your telemedicine experience with these simple tricks.

Find a quiet, well-lit space

Ensure the doctor can see and hear you clearly by sitting facing a window or lamp, away from background noise.

Write down your symptoms beforehand

It's easy to forget things during an appointment. Keep a list of your symptoms, current medications, and questions ready.

Check your connection

Join the waiting room 5 minutes early to test your camera, microphone, and internet stability.
